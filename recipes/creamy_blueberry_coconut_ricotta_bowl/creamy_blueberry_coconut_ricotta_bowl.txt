Title: Creamy Blueberry Coconut Ricotta Bowl
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Description: This filling breakfast bowl will jumpstart your day and keep you full all morning. It is chock full of protein and is super delicious and creamy.
Ingredients: [
    "¼ cup whole milk ricotta cheese",
    "1 tablespoon coconut milk",
    "1 tablespoon honey, or to taste",
    "1 tablespoon slivered almonds",
    "1 tablespoon sweetened flaked coconut",
    "½ cup blueberries, or to taste"
]
Description: [
    "Combine ricotta cheese and coconut milk in a bowl. Drizzle on honey and sprinkle with almonds and coconut. Top with blueberries."
]
